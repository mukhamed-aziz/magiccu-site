Magic Cube Simulator

Privacy Policy & Support · Developer: Magiccu · Effective date: July 12, 2026

The short version: Magic Cube Simulator collects no personal data. No accounts, no ads, no analytics, no tracking, no data leaves your device.

Privacy Policy

1. Data we collect

None. Magic Cube Simulator does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data or usage data. The game contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, no social integrations, and requires no account or sign-in.

2. Data stored on your device

Your game progress — cube state, move history, solve records, and settings (such as sound volume, language, and graphics quality) — is saved locally on your device only. It is never uploaded anywhere. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes this data.

3. Internet access

The game is fully playable offline and does not transmit personal data over the internet.

4. Platform services

Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play) may collect diagnostic information — such as opt-in crash reports or install statistics — under their own privacy policies and your device settings. That collection is controlled by Apple/Google, not by us, and we receive at most aggregated, anonymous versions of it. See Apple's Privacy Policy and Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Children

The game is suitable for general audiences. Because we collect no data from anyone, we also collect no data from children.

6. Changes to this policy

If the app ever changes in a way that affects this policy (for example, adding online features), the updated policy will be posted at this address with a new effective date before the change ships.
