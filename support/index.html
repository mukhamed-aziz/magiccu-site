Writing a bug report

You don't have to do any of this, but it helps us fix things faster:

What to include

Your phone model and OS version, what you were doing when it happened, and whether it happens every time or just once.

A screenshot or a short screen recording is worth a hundred words, especially for anything visual. If the game froze or something looked wrong, a recording usually tells us the cause immediately.

Where to find the version number

Open the menu, tap Credits, and look at the bottom. It reads something like v1.0.0 (260716.011256). Include that line and we'll know exactly which build you have.

Common questions

Does the game work offline?

Yes, completely. There's no account, no sign-in, and nothing to download after you install it. Airplane mode makes no difference.

Is my progress saved if I close the game?

Yes. Your cube, your move history, and your records are saved on your device as you play, so you can pick up exactly where you left off. Each cube size keeps its own progress and its own best times.

I changed phones. Can I bring my records with me?

No, and this is a deliberate trade-off. Everything is stored on your device and nothing is uploaded anywhere, which is why the game needs no account and collects no data about you. The cost of that is there's no cloud copy to restore from.

How do I undo a move, or start over?

Undo takes back one move at a time, and there's no limit to how far back you can go.

Reset rewinds the entire solve in reverse, all the way back to the scramble you started from. If you'd rather not watch the whole rewind, a double tap skips to the end.

Is there a tutorial or a solver?

No, and there won't be. The whole idea of the game is that you work the cube out yourself. There are no hints, no step-by-step guides, and no button that solves it for you.

The Controls page in the menu shows you how the gestures work. That's how to handle the cube, not how to solve it.

The game is warm or the battery drains quickly

Open Settings and set Graphics to LOW or MID. AUTO picks a level for your device, but a manual setting always wins. Lower levels ask much less of the phone.

Can I turn off vibration or sound?

Yes, both. Settings has Haptics (off, subtle, full) and a Sounds toggle with its own volume slider.

Can I change the language?

Yes. Settings has a Language picker with 13 languages. It follows your phone's language by default, and you can override it at any time.

How do I delete my data?

The menu has a Reset screen (it's its own row, next to Settings) where you can clear your personal records, your saved cubes and progress, or everything at once. Uninstalling the app also removes all of it permanently.

What data do you collect about me?

None. No accounts, no ads, no analytics, no tracking. The privacy policy spells it out.
