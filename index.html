The game

Built around how a cube should feel

Layers glide under your finger, snap into place with a satisfying spring, and every solve ends in a celebration you earned.

Four cube sizes

Warm up on the 2×2, then work up to the 5×5. Each size keeps its own best times.

Tactile controls

Drag to turn a layer. Flick to spin the whole cube. Swipe with two fingers and two layers turn at once.

Timer & records

The timer sits quietly at the top. Beat one of your three best times and you get fireworks for it.

Unlimited undo

Take back one move, or every move. Reset replays your whole solve in reverse, and a double tap skips to the end.

Zen aesthetic

Dark, slow, quiet. Nothing on screen blinks for your attention while you think.

Rich haptics

Every turn and snap has its own vibration. Dial it back or switch it off in settings.
